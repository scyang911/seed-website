Location

Getting Here
🚇 By MRT
Take the Wenhu Line (Brown Line) to Technology Building Station (科技大樓站). Exit 3, then walk ~5 minutes north along Keelung Road.
🚌 By Bus
Routes 1, 3, 72, 207, 295, 298, 672 – alight at NTUST (台科大) stop on Keelung Road Sec. 4.
🚗 By Car / Taxi
Enter campus via the main gate on Keelung Road Sec. 4. Visitor parking is available in the campus parking structure (fee applies).
🏢 Within Campus
The International Building (國際大樓) is located near the main gate. SEED Center is on the 6th floor, Room 606.
