🌱 3rd SEED Symposium & SEED Master Talk 2026

Bringing together SEED researchers, postdoctoral fellows, and graduate students to present and discuss their latest research developments.

Research Topics Presented
  • ⚡ Anode-free lithium metal batteries
  • 🔋 Lithium–sulfur batteries
  • 🧪 Solid-state & polymer electrolytes
  • ⚗️ Argyrodite solid electrolytes
  • 🔬 Electro-chemo-mechanical behavior in batteries
  • 📊 Graphene-based cathode materials
  • 💧 Electrocatalysts for hydrogen evolution & H₂O₂ production
  • 🌿 Ammonia electrosynthesis pathways
  • 🌊 Anion-exchange membrane water electrolysis
  • 🤖 ML/AI-assisted battery modeling & lithium plating prediction
