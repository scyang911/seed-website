Battery Safety & Thermal Analysis

Thermal runaway detection, microcalorimetry, and thermophysical characterization

★ Flagship
Battery Thermal Runaway Monitoring System
THT EV+ARC
World-class EV battery safety testing instrument using Accelerating Rate Calorimetry (ARC) to precisely simulate thermal runaway onset conditions and propagation behavior, quantifying battery thermal stability and safety margins.
  • Sensitivity 0.05 °C/min — detects weak self-heating onset
  • Pressure range 0–200 bar, resolution 0.005 bar
  • Compliant with SAE J2464, USABC/FreedomCAR, UN/UL standards
  • Applicable from cell to module scale testing
★ Flagship
Multi-Channel Isothermal Calorimeter
TA Instruments TAM IV
High-precision 8-channel isothermal calorimeter capable of simultaneously measuring µW-level heat flow, enabling long-term monitoring of battery aging rates, self-discharge heat generation, and solid electrolyte synthesis thermodynamics.
  • Temperature control precision ±0.0001 °C (patented thermostatic bath)
  • Up to 8 independent channels measured simultaneously
  • Detection limit at µW-level heat flow
  • Three modes: isothermal, step-isothermal, temperature scanning
★ Flagship
High-Performance Modulated DSC
TA Discovery DSC 2500
High-sensitivity Modulated DSC (MDSC) separating reversible and non-reversible thermal events, widely used for glass transition, melting, and crystallization analysis of solid electrolytes, electrode materials, and polymer separators.
  • Temperature range −90 °C to 550 °C
  • MDSC separates complex overlapping thermal events
  • Tzero® technology ensures baseline stability and high sensitivity
  • Compatible with RCS cooling accessory for sub-ambient measurements

In-Situ Electrochemical Characterization & Gas Analysis

Real-time reaction visualization and off-gas monitoring during charge/discharge

★ Flagship
In-Situ Electrochemical Confocal Visualization System
Lasertec ECCS B320
A globally rare in-situ electrochemical confocal microscopy system enabling real-time, high-resolution observation of electrode surface reactions, dendrite growth, and active material volume expansion during battery cycling — a core tool for failure mechanism studies.
  • True-color confocal optics eliminate electrolyte interface interference
  • Quantifies electrode thickness changes and active material distribution
  • Tracks graphite anode state-of-charge color shifts in real time
  • Integrated potentiostat for synchronized current/voltage recording
★ Flagship
Fast Scanning Confocal Raman Microscope
Nanobase XperRAM-S
High-performance multi-laser confocal Raman microscope supporting rapid 2D area scanning for non-destructive chemical structure and phase distribution analysis of battery electrodes and solid electrolytes at nano-to-micro scale.
  • Multiple laser wavelengths (405–1064 nm) for diverse material systems
  • Fast 2D area scanning (200×200 µm)
  • Multiple gratings (300–2400 lpmm) with switchable high-resolution mode
  • Supports photoluminescence (PL) and electroluminescence (EL) measurements
★ Flagship
GC-MS Battery Off-Gas Analysis Platform
Shimadzu GC-MS
Integrated GC-MS platform for precise identification of trace gases evolved during battery charging, over-charge, or thermal runaway events (CO₂, CO, H₂, hydrocarbons) — a critical tool for electrolyte decomposition mechanism and battery safety studies.
  • GC-MS coupling provides both separation (GC) and identification (MS)
  • Detects trace gases at ppm to ppb concentration levels
  • Integrated with electrochemical workstation for in-situ gas analysis
  • Used for electrolyte decomposition and solid electrolyte interphase research

Materials Microstructure & Surface Analysis

Nanoscale morphology, elemental composition, surface area and pore size characterization

★ Flagship
Scanning Electron Microscope with EDS
JEOL JSM-IT200 + EDS
High-resolution SEM integrated with Energy Dispersive X-ray Spectroscopy (EDS) for simultaneous nanoscale morphology and elemental mapping of electrodes, solid electrolytes, and separators, supporting both high-vacuum and low-vacuum (non-conductive) modes.
  • Resolution better than 10 nm (high-vacuum mode, 30 kV)
  • Accelerating voltage 0.5–30 kV adjustable
  • EDS real-time point analysis, line scan, and elemental mapping
  • Low-vacuum mode for uncoated non-conductive specimens
★ Flagship
Chemisorption Analyzer
Micromeritics AutoChem 2920
Fully automated temperature-programmed chemisorption analyzer for measuring active site counts, metal dispersion, and acid/base properties of electrocatalysts via TPR/TPO/TPD experiments — providing key data for HER/OER catalyst development.
  • Supports TPR (Temperature-Programmed Reduction), TPO, and TPD
  • Measures metal dispersion and active surface area
  • High-precision TCD detector with high sensitivity
  • Fully automated for continuous multi-sample operation
★ Flagship
Automated BET Surface Area & Pore Size Analyzer
Micromeritics ASAP 2020
Industry-standard BET surface area and pore size distribution system using nitrogen physisorption isotherms to precisely measure surface area, pore size distribution, and pore volume of electrode materials, solid electrolytes, and porous structures.
  • Pore size analysis range 3.5 Å–5000 Å (mesopore to macropore)
  • High-precision N₂ adsorption, surface area accuracy better than ±1.5%
  • Supports multi-point BET and BJH pore size distribution analysis
  • Dual sample preparation stations for improved throughput

Materials Fabrication & Battery Assembly

From composite compounding and thin film preparation to inert-environment battery assembly

★ Flagship
Micro High-Torque Twin-Screw Compounder
Xplore Instruments MC 15 HT
Dutch-made high-end micro twin-screw compounder achieving homogeneous compounding of composite solid electrolytes, polymer electrodes, or polymer composites with as little as 15 mL sample volume — ideal for early-stage R&D with precious materials.
  • Minimum sample volume of just 15 mL, conserving precious materials
  • High torque up to 43 Nm for high-viscosity materials
  • Maximum operating temperature 450 °C with three heating zones
  • Speed range 1–500 rpm; Vari-Batch™ variable volume design
★ Flagship
Face-to-Face Electrospinning System
FALCO (Falco Tech Enterprise)
Taiwan-made needle-to-needle electrospinning machine dedicated to fabricating PAA (polyamic acid) and PI (polyimide) high-performance nanofiber separators. The face-to-face design generates a uniform electric field for precise control of fiber diameter and membrane thickness.
  • Face-to-face design with uniform electric field and consistent fiber quality
  • Operating voltage 15–30 kV; feed rate 0.1–0.8 mL/h
  • Produces PAA/PI separators with high thermal stability and mechanical strength
  • Separators enhance battery thermal stability and safety performance
★ Flagship
Glovebox Workstation System (6-Unit Cluster)
M. Braun Inert Gas Systems
German M. Braun glovebox system with 6 interconnected units forming a large inert gas (Ar) enclosed workspace for Li/Na metal battery assembly, sulfide solid electrolyte synthesis, and processing — rigorously excluding moisture and oxygen contamination.
  • H₂O and O₂ controlled below 0.1 ppm
  • 6 interconnected gloveboxes for spacious working environment
  • Integrated recirculation purification system for continuous inert atmosphere
  • Covers full workflow: metal Li anodes, solid electrolyte assembly

🏭
Special Facility

Dry Room (Low-Humidity Facility)

SEED Center's large-scale low-humidity workspace located in IB1202, with dew point controllable to below −75 °C. It serves as the core facility for batch fabrication of coin cells, pouch cells, and sulfide solid-state batteries, and as a training space for students to learn industrial battery manufacturing processes.
